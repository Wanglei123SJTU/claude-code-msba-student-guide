Projects

01

Assignment 1: Consumer Segmentation via PCA and KMeans

Analyzed 30 respondents across five store-preference variables (X1-X5). PCA on standardized inputs retained two components: PC1 = 53.43% and PC2 = 37.04% (90.48% cumulative). KMeans on the PC1-PC2 map supported three segments (best silhouette = 0.609): Discount-Oriented Pragmatists (43.33%), Independent Department-Store Browsers (20.00%), and Service-Seeking Quality Shoppers (36.67%).
